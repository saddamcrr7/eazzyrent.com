What Is EazzyRent PMS

We deliver the software you need to take your business to the next level.

The days of switching between excel, word and your accounting software are over. Discover efficiency, speed and convenience in a unified platform. What you get from EazzyRent:

Core Pms
Create landlords, properties, units and tenants. Invoice for rent, service charge, utility bills and repairs. Create landlord and tenant statements all these with simple intuitive steps. Repetitive tasks are automated.

Accounting
Your accounting transactions are recorded in real time as you transact. Manage company expenses, suppliers and other transactions. Prepare for auditing and returns by easily generating relevant reports such as trial balance, income statement and balance sheet.

EazzyRent Benefits

Online based
You can access the system from anywhere, any time and using any browser based device. Centralize your operations on a cloud platform that can scale as your business grows.
Secure
Our hardware and performance testing, 24/7 system monitoring combined with stringent security measures ensure downtime is minimized and your data is always safe.
Recurrent Tasks
Don't sweat the small stuff. EazzyRent will automate all your recurrent tasks such as invoicing tenants, unit rates adjustments, reminders and penalties for late rent.
Online Rent Processing
Collect rent faster by taking advantage of integrations to your paybill and bank accounts. Rent is received automatically and receipt sent to the tenant via sms and email in real time.
Bulk sms & Email
Communicate with tenants, landlords, staff members and other stake holders. Send receipts, invoices, statements and reminders.
Debt Control
EazzyRent provides you with tools to keep an eye on your tenants debt levels such as reminders, demand notes, quit notice and a workflow to assign debt recovery tasks to different stake holders.
Landlord Statements
Prepare landlord statements for the month at the click of a button. Capture expenses such as repairs, advances, loans, your management commission and see them reflect in the statement.
Accounts
Have a clear view of the health of your business without having to worry if the books are in order. Your transactions will be posted accurately and in real time to correct ledgers.
Document Creation
Spend less time on paper work. The system will auto generate landlord agreements, lease documents, notice to vacate, demand letters, quit notice and other documents.
Marketing
We advertise your vacant units on eazzyrent.com and remove them when occupied. Unlock the full potential of your business through analytics and insights.
Reports
Get a snapshot of what is happening through charts and graphs. Narrow information to what you specifically need from a wide array of available reports.
Support
Get support when you need it. We provide you with multiple support channels such as phone calls, email, QA and chat to keep your business moving.
